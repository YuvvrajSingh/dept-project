**Speed matters for adoption.**

## Objection Handling

**Objection: vmedulife has more features**
> True. But do you use 80% of ERP features? EduSched gives you the 20% you actually need - at 5% of the cost.

**Objection: vmedulife is more established**
> We understand. That's why we offer free beta for first 3 colleges. Let the product speak for itself.

**Objection: We might grow and need more features**
> Fair point. As you grow, we can add modules. And at ₹75K base, you still save lakhs even if you upgrade later.

**Objection: AI sounds better than algorithm**
> Both achieve conflict-free scheduling. The label doesn't matter. What matters is results - and our auto-scheduler works.
